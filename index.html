Decentralized music streaming

A decentralized platform for musicians to upload music, without censorship, fraud, or unfair treatment.

Powered with Ethereum

Own your Content.

Powering a new era of content economy with blockchain technology

DEMU is a decentralized Platform for musicians to own their content!

With the rise of centralized corporations controlling most of the information dissemination on the internet, a decentralized platform is needed to give creators a place they can own it.

Simple use

This platform will allow creators to directly and easily monetize content. This is the first of its kind that enables such a powerful interaction between creators and listeners.

No distribution fees

When a listener only chooses to listen to one song in a given month, the entire subscription cost is transferred to the single musician. The subscription is always distributed fairly based on listeners activity.

Push your music.

Be discovered by a community of people for marketing purposes by using DEMU Discovery.
